Common questions

  • How do I invite family? Open the Family tab and tap “Invite more family” to share a link.
  • Reminders aren't arriving? Make sure notifications are enabled for PetRelay in your iOS Settings.
  • Managing your subscription: Family Pro is managed in your Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Delete your account: Settings → Delete account, inside the app.
